About Me

Hello, I'm Styles, a Data Analyst passionate about turning raw data into meaningful insights that drive smarter decisions. I enjoy working with data to uncover trends, solve problems, and present information in a way that is clear, practical, and impactful.

My analytical toolkit includes SQL, Python, Microsoft Excel, and Power BI. I use these tools to clean, transform, analyze, and visualize data, helping businesses and individuals better understand their performance and opportunities.

I have experience working on projects involving data cleaning, exploratory data analysis, dashboard development, and business intelligence reporting. Through these projects, I have developed strong problem-solving skills and a keen eye for identifying patterns hidden within complex datasets.

I believe that data is most valuable when it leads to action. That is why I focus not only on analyzing numbers but also on communicating insights effectively through reports, visualizations, and data storytelling.

As a lifelong learner, I continue to expand my expertise in analytics, business intelligence, and emerging data technologies. My goal is to help organizations make confident, data-driven decisions that create measurable results.
